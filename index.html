CharlesLomax& Son Ltd

Nottinghams finest builders and DIY merchants - Est. 1890

Established in 1890, Charles Lomax & Sons are professional builders merchants providing building material and delivery services to customers across the Nottingham area. We supply a wide range of products including timber, plaster, aggregates, fencing and hardware. We also deal with industrial gas, Calor gas and plumbing materials. Our clients are trade and DIY customers including schools, local authorities and building companies. We sell all our building materials at trade prices and offer a fast and reliable local delivery service.
